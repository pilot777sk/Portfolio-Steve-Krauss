About Me

Steve Krauss was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan and always loved fixing things. He built his own home at age 22 in Oxford Michigan. At 21 he started a long career as an automotive design engineer. One of his passions was aviation. He became a pilot and later did a career change and worked for the airlines. When he moved to the Seattle area in 2008, he became more interested in technology.

In the fall of 2017, Steve wanted to continue with some type of engineering/technology and searched web programing. Steve found the Full Stack Web development course at UCF. Steve decided to to take advantage of his engineering skills and change his career for the third time. With that said, Steve has a very diverse background. His new knowlege of Full Stack Web Development will round out his skills and continue using his creativity to excite his customers.

Steve's new skills include both Frontend and Backend coding in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, MySQL, Handlebars, Express.js, node.js ReactJS, & MongoDB.
